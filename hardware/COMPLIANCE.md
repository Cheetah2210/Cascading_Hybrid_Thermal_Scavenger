# Hardware Compliance & Material Standards

- **Mechanical Chassis:** Designed for high-thermal-flux environments. Use vacuum-compatible materials (e.g., Grade 5 Titanium or 316L Stainless) for space-rated iterations.
- **Ceramic Vane Profiles:** Optimized for reduced edge-current shunting.
- **Electrical Safety:** All RF electrode control circuits are designed for EMI-shielded containment to ensure compatibility with sensitive server rack and spacecraft telemetry.
