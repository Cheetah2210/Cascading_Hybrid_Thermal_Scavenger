# Hardware Compliance and Decommissioning Statement: GEN-III CHTS

## 1. Decommissioning of Legacy Systems
As of the current operational cycle, all Magnetohydrodynamic (MHD), plasma-injection, and high-frequency RF-based thermal systems are strictly prohibited and decommissioned. All associated hardware components have been removed.

## 2. Solid-State Compliance Standards
The GEN-III 4-Stage Thermal Cascade (CHTS) complies with the following safety and efficiency standards:
- **Thermal Management:** System is strictly limited to sensible thermal energy scavenging.
- **Conversion Modules:** Solid-state Seebeck modules (TEG) and zeotropic heat exchangers must operate within the 200kW thermal capacity limit.
- **Containment:** Phase Change Material (PCM) secondary storage containers are pressurized-rated and undergo annual integrity inspections.

## 3. Regulatory Certification
All hardware assemblies meet non-ionizing radiation safety standards, as no electromagnetic fields are utilized in this solid-state design.
