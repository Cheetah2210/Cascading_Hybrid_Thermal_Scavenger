# Mechanical Tolerance and Assembly: GEN-III CHTS

## 1. Assembly Tolerances
- **Thermal Contact Interface:** Interface pressure must be maintained at 1.5 MPa across all TEG semiconductor surfaces to minimize interfacial thermal resistance.
- **Zeotropic Loop:** All fittings are sealed to vacuum-grade standards to prevent loss of binary fluids.

## 2. Structural Integrity
- Assemblies are bolted using thermal expansion-compensated fasteners to prevent warping during peak 200kW load conditions.
