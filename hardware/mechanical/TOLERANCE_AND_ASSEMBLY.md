# CHTS Assembly & Tolerance Specifications

## 1. Ceramic Vane Precision
- **Material:** Yttria-Stabilized Zirconia (YSZ) for high-temperature creep resistance.
- **Surface Roughness:** Ra 0.4 μm required on the pressure side to maintain laminar flow at 45 m/s.
- **Interference Fit:** Vane roots must be shrunk-fit into the nickel-superalloy housing at 400°C to ensure zero-leakage at operating temperature.

## 2. Housing Structural Integrity
- **Thermal Expansion Gap:** The inner scavenger hood requires a 0.2mm thermal expansion allowance relative to the outer manifold casing to prevent buckling at 1200K.
- **Pressure Seal:** Graphite-impregnated metallic O-rings rated for 15 bar continuous pressure.
