# Ceramic Vane Profile Specifications

## Geometry Logic
The vanes are designed with a modified NACA 6-series airfoil profile to minimize boundary layer separation during high-velocity MHD vapor flow.

## Manufacturing Parameters
- **Material:** Silicon Carbide (SiC) or Zirconia-Toughened Alumina (ZTA) for thermal shock resistance.
- **Tolerance:** +/- 0.05mm for high-pressure seal interfaces.
- **Surface Finish:** Ra 0.8 μm to reduce Hartmann drag coefficient.

## File Assets
- `scavenger_hood_v03.STEP`: Main chassis housing.
- `vane_array_manifold.STEP`: Precision-aligned vane assembly.
