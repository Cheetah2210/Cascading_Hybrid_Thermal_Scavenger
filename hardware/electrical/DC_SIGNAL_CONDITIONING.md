# DC Signal Conditioning: GEN-III CHTS

## 1. Overview
The DC signal conditioning unit converts the fluctuating low-voltage DC generated by the TEG modules into a regulated high-efficiency output.

## 2. Technical Specifications
- **Input Range:** 0.5V to 24V DC per module string.
- **Conversion Topology:** High-efficiency buck-boost DC-DC converter.
- **Filtering:** LC low-pass filter stage to mitigate ripple current from the Seebeck junction switching.
- **Protection:** Galvanic isolation to prevent reverse-flow currents during low-thermal-gradient periods.

## 3. Operational Requirements
- Calibration required every 6 months to adjust for internal series resistance changes in the TEG semiconductor materials.
