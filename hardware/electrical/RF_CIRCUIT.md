# RF Ionization Control Circuit (Stage 1)

## Architecture
This module drives the plasma ionization flux required for high-conductivity MHD operation.

## Specifications
- **Frequency:** 13.56 MHz (Standard ISM band).
- **Matching Network:** Automated PI-network to account for plasma impedance shifts.
- **Cooling Requirements:** Passive forced-air cooling for the MOSFET heat sink.
