# Safety and Regulatory Guidelines: CHTS GEN-III

## 1. Thermal Cascade Operational Safety
- **Input Limit:** The system is hard-capped at 200kW input capacity. Exceeding this limit triggers an automated safety shunt to the secondary PCM buffer.
- **Thermal Stress Mitigation:** PCM buffering prevents rapid thermal cycling, mitigating the risk of structural micro-fractures in the semiconductor junctions.
- **Emergency Shutdown:** Rapid temperature spikes (> 150°C) initiate a total zeotropic loop bypass to protect the adsorption bed integrity.

## 2. Secondary Storage Handling (PCM)
- **Containment:** PCM thermal buffers must be housed in reinforced, high-thermal-conductivity containers.
- **Chemical Safety:** PCM materials must be handled as per the localized SDS (Safety Data Sheet) for non-toxic salt hydrates or organic paraffin bases.

## 3. Maintenance Cycles
- **Quarterly:** Inspection of RTD sensor calibration and flow meter functionality.
- **Annually:** Integrity scan of the PCM containment vessels and vacuum-seal check on the adsorption beds.
