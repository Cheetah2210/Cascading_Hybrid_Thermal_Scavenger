# CHTS Safety & Compliance Standards

## Thermal Management
- **Emergency Quench:** The system must include a secondary nitrogen-injection valve for rapid cooling of the TEG sandwich should $T_{hot}$ exceed 1350K.
- **Redundancy:** Dual redundant thermocouples (Type-K or Type-N) for real-time monitoring of the Stage 1 exhaust.

## Environmental
- **Zeotropic Fluid Safety:** The Stage 3 zeotropic mixture must use non-ozone-depleting agents (GWP < 1). MSDS sheets for the specific mixture must be strictly filed in the `/docs/safety/` directory.
