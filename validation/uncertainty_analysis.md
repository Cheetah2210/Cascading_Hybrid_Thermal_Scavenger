# Thermal Uncertainty Analysis: GEN-III CHTS

## 1. Measurement Error Budget
- **Temperature Sensors (RTDs):** ±0.1°C calibration tolerance.
- **Heat Flux (TEG Modules):** ±2.5% variation due to semiconductor contact resistance.
- **Coolant Flow:** ±1.0% variation in zeotropic loop mass-flow rate.

## 2. Systemic Variance
Total energy balance variance is modeled at ±3.2% per 100kW of input. MHD fluid instabilities are no longer a factor in this solid-state model.
