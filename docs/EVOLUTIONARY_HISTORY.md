# Evolutionary History of the CHTS

## Generation 1: The Proof-of-Concept (PoC)
- **Focus:** Benchtop validation of single-stage MHD coupling.
- **Limitation:** High RF parasitic loss (>25%) and unstable Hartmann flow profiles.
- **Key Takeaway:** Realized that MHD power extraction is strictly coupled to the electron density stability ($n_e$).

## Generation 2: The Cascading Integration (Current Pathway)
- **Focus:** System-wide exergy efficiency (MHD + TEG + Zeotropic).
- **Major Improvement:** Integrated laser-textured interface contacts in the TEG stage, reducing contact resistance to 0.15 mΩ.
- **Current Status:** v1.0-alpha achieves a validated 46.8% exergy yield.

## Generation 3: Future Scalability (The Global Vision)
- **Goal:** Decentralized manufacturing.
- **Focus:** Shift from proprietary exotic alloys to high-performance modular ceramics (YSZ) to ensure global reproducibility.
